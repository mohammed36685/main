Display

Foldable Dynamic AMOLED 2X 7.6-inch hole-in-the-face display comes in QXGA+ 1768×2208 pixels at 373p per inch and the screen supports HDR10+ mode and screen frequency rate 1 20Hz and when folded comes with a second screen and comes from super AMOLED type with a size of 6.23 inches in 816×2260 pixels and the screen supports display dimensions by 25:9 to display more content than videos and games on the screen with corning gorilla protection layer Glass Victus and phone back come from Gorilla Glass 6 glass and the screen supports the ability to split into two via multi Active Window to do more than one order at a time.

Camera

The front camera comes with a 10-megapixel F/2.2 aperture. The front camera in the cover comes in a 10-megapixel resolution with an F/2.2 aperture. The front camera supports 4K 2,160p video shooting at a rate of 30 frames per second and supports 1080p FHD shooting at a rate of 30 frames per second. The rear camera comes a little prominent on the back of the phone and comes with a triple camera where the first camera comes in 12 megapixels with an F/1.8 aperture, which is the primary camera and supports the OIS optical stabilizer, and the second camera comes in a 12-megapixel resolution with an F//1 lens aperture. 2.4 It is the zoom camera that supports the optical stabilizer and supports a real Zoom 2X, while the third camera comes in a 12-megapixel resolution with the F/2.2 lens aperture, which is for wide shooting as well as a single led flash. The rear camera supports 4K 2,160p video shooting at 30 and 60 frames per second and supports 1080p FHD shooting at 60 and 240 frames per second.

Rear camera

The rear camera supports 4K 2,160p video shooting at 30 and 60 frames per second, supports 1,080p FHD shooting at 60 and 240 frames per second and supports 720p HD shooting at 960p/s capture rate.

Battery

The battery comes with a capacity of 4500 mAh. The battery supports 25W fast charging and supports 11W wireless charging as well as 4.5W reverse wireless charging support.
